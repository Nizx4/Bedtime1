Milo the Moon Mouse

A lunar tale of courage

Milo was a tiny mouse who lived on the moon. He loved to nibble on moon cheese and watch the Earth twinkle in the distance. But Milo was shy and wished he had a friend to share his adventures.

One night, he spotted a shooting star and made a wish for a companion. Suddenly, a little space bunny landed beside him! Together, Milo and his new friend bounced across craters, played hide and seek, and shared moon cheese snacks.

Milo learned that sometimes, all you need is a little courage—and a wish upon a star—to find a friend.

The end.
