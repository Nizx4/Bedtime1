Tilly the Turtle's Dream

A gentle journey under the sea

Tilly was a little turtle who loved to dream big. Every night, she imagined herself swimming to the farthest corners of the ocean, meeting magical sea creatures and finding hidden treasures.

One night, Tilly dreamed she was the fastest swimmer in the sea! She raced dolphins, danced with jellyfish, and even helped a lost seahorse find its way home.

When Tilly woke up, she realized that dreams can take you anywhere—and that every adventure starts with a single dream.

The end.
